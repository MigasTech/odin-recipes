Polvo à Lagareiro

Description of the recipe

Polvo à Lagareiro is undoubtedly one of the most typical dishes in Portuguese cuisine. The term lagareiro represents the olive oil mill workers from the producing areas. Hence the name of the dish that uses olive oil in large quantities.The recipe consists of boiled octopus baked in the oven, drizzled with a lot of olive oil, accompanied by garlic, onion, and potatoes.

List of ingredients

  • 1 octopus (about 1.5 kg)
  • 500 g small potatoes for roasting
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 200ml olive oil (especially the portuguese brands)
  • 1 bay life
  • Salt at your taste

Steps to make it:

  1. Place the octopus in a pot, cover with water, season with salt, the bay leaf and the whole onion in its shell and cook for 45 minutes.
  2. Wash the potatoes well with the peel, drain them and arrange them on a baking tray. Sprinkle them with coarse salt and bake in the oven, preheated to 180ºc, for about 40 minutes.
  3. Após o tempo indicado, escorra o polvo e disponha-o num tabuleiro. Junte os alhos esmagados com casca e regue com o azeite. Leve também ao forno e deixe assar por cerca de 15 minutos.
  4. Retire as batatas do forno, dê um "murro" a cada uma e sirva-as com o polvo, envolvendo tudo com o azeite quente e os alhos.
